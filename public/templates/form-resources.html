Useful Resources

Eviction Defence Collaborative

What it is good for? EDC is a drop-in legal clinic that helps with low-income tenants to respond to eviction lawsuits. You can find out more information on their website HERE. Their hours are Mon - Fri 9:30AM-11:30AM, 1PM-3PM and their office address is: 995 Market Street #1200 San Francisco, CA 94103

Eviction Process Flowchart

What it is good for? The site page provides a general eviction process notice chart. Actual procedures in San Francisco might be different. View chart HERE.

San Francisco Tenants Union

What it is good for? The tenants union provides general information and counseling services to tenents in San Francisco. Visit their website HERE. And their office address is: 558 Capp Street San Francisco, CA 94110

Housing Rights Committee of San Francisco

What it is good for? The housing rights committee can help you if you live in San Francisco and you're having a problem (such as an illegal rent increase, evictions, roommate problems, mold and other repair issues, etc.), the committee can try and help you. Visit their website HERE. Their counseling hours are Mon-Thurs 1-5pm and office address is: 417 South Van Ness San Francisco, CA 94103

City & County of San Francisco Rent Board

What it is good for? The San Francisco Rent Ordinance regulates rents and evictions for certain residential rental units in San Francisco. The Rent Board's primary function is to conduct hearings and mediations of tenant and landlord petitions regarding the adjustment of rents under the City's rent control laws. Visit their website HERE. They have weekday counseling hours 8AM - 5PM.
